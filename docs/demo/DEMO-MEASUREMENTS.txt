MEASUREMENT 5: MAXIMUM RESULTS (All 20 Sinks)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Best for: Showing the FULL catalog - matches every sink

  Cabinet Width:          40"
  Cabinet Depth:          26"
  Cabinet Height:         34"
  Countertop Material:    quartz
  Countertop Thickness:   1.5"
  Mounting Style:         (leave blank - no preference)
  Existing Bowl Count:    (leave blank - no preference)

Expected matches: ALL 20 SINKS in catalog

Why this works:
  - 40" width fits largest sink (36") with 4" clearance
  - 26" depth fits deepest sink (22") with 4" clearance
  - No mounting style preference = no penalty for any style
  - No bowl count preference = all bowl counts score equally

SINK DIMENSIONS BY MOUNTING STYLE:

  Mounting Style    |  Widths Available   |  Depths Available
  ------------------|---------------------|-------------------
  Undermount        |  28", 30", 32", 33" |  18", 19", 22"
  Drop-in           |  25", 33"           |  20", 22"

  Flush Mount       |  30", 32"           |  18", 19"


FITTING FORMULA:

  - Cabinet width should be AT LEAST 4" wider than sink width
  - Cabinet depth should be AT LEAST 3" deeper than sink depth
  - This yields "excellent" fit ratings in the matching algorithm

================================================================================
                              DEMO TIPS
================================================================================

1. Start with Measurement 1 (Standard Kitchen) - shows the most results

2. For price variety, the catalog ranges from:
   - Budget:  $199.99 (Classic Drop-In 25")
   - Mid:     $449.99 (ProChef Double Bowl 33")
   - Premium: $1,299.99 (Artisan Copper Farmhouse 30")

3. Avoid creating quotes while offline during demo

4. The matching algorithm scores based on:
   - Width fit (30 points max)
   - Depth fit (30 points max)
   - Mounting style match (25 points max)
   - Bowl count match (15 points max)
